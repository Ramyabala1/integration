e-Manas Karnataka (Mental Healthcare Management System)

Glance over e-Manas Karnataka
e-Manas Karnataka (Mental Healthcare Management System) is an internet-based Karnataka state-wide digital registry of mental health establishments (MHEs), mental health professionals (MHPs), people with mental illnesses (PwMIs) and their treatment records. This platform helps establishments and professionals ensure compliance with Mental Healthcare Act 2017. This platform is being developed as a National Health Mission initiative under Karnataka Government, in collaboration with NIMHANS and E-Health Research Center of IIIT Bangalore.
